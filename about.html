I'm Nicolas Jaureguiberry,

I am a professional photographer, born on September 7th, based in Quilmes, Buenos Aires.
I began my studies in photography in 2011 at ISEC, graduating with a solid technical foundation.
With over 10 years of experience as a professional, I have found a way to communicate
and capture unique, authentic, and expressive moments through photography.

I specialize in portraits for bands and in photography for social and corporate events.
I am passionate about capturing the essence and dynamism of each subject and context,
creating images full of life.

I also work to strengthen my clients' personal brands through photography,
highlighting their unique identity and values. Each image is an authentic
reflection of their style and presence.
